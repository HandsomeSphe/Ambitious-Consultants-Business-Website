About Us

Our Values

Ambitious Business Consultants core value proposition is to assist emerging contractors by identifying, planning and implementing operational performance improvement initiatives, in order to achieve operational performance excellence.Major benefits include:

Increased professionalism increased revenue reduced costs improved customer experience improved compliance and control

Our Mission

Ambitious Business Consultants is dedicated to to providing performance improvement consulting services that assist emerging contractors to develop and optimize their business operations. We would like to establish a successful partnership with Metropolitan Municipality and TVET colleges by being a consulting firm that will offer employment opportunities to graduates in order to solve business management, administration and compliance problems which are existing in construction sector particularly in emerging contractors.

Our Vision

To be a valued Performance Improvement consultancy in South Africa, across multiple industries. To be recognized as excellent and effective leaders in business consulting in the whole of Southern Africa, in both public and private sector enterprises.
